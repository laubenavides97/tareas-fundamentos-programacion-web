Welcome to Cloud Vista Lodge

Cloud Vista Lodge is a charming mountain hotel located in the heart of Santa María de Dota, Costa Rica. Surrounded by a lush landscape of mountains and cloud forests, this retreat offers a peaceful and welcoming atmosphere, perfect for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Discover our suites and villas

We offer you a unique lodging experience in our cozy individual villas and elegant suites in the main building. Each space has been designed to provide you with maximum comfort and privacy, surrounded by the stunning natural beauty of Santa María de Dota.

Our villas, ideal for those seeking a tranquil getaway, are equipped with all modern amenities, while the suites in the main building blend luxury and warmth in a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you wish to enjoy a spectacular view from your balcony or relax by the fireplace, each lodging option is crafted to make your stay a memorable experience.

Discover the perfect mountain retreat and immerse yourself in the peace and serenity that only Cloud Vista Lodge can offer!

Enjoy your stay

At Cloud Vista Lodge, adventure and a connection with nature await you at every turn. We offer a variety of activities designed for all preferences and experience levels, ensuring every guest can fully enjoy the beauty of Santa María de Dota.

Embark on exciting horseback rides through breathtaking landscapes, explore trails surrounded by unique flora and fauna on our guided hikes, or push your limits with an exhilarating canopy experience that lets you soar over the forest. Additionally, night hikes offer the chance to discover the magic of the surroundings under the starlit mountain sky.

Whether you're a nature enthusiast or a thrill-seeker, Cloud Vista Lodge has the perfect activity to make your stay an unforgettable experience. Come and embrace the adventure in the heart of the mountains!
